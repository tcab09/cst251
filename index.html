Tatiana Cabrera

About Me!

Tatiana Cabrera is a 3rd year CD student at CSUMB with a focus in visual comminication design.

Where is She From?

She has lived in the beautiful Monterey Peninsula all her life and just recently moved to Carmel Valley. The change in location has really insprired her creative mind and she feels very fortunate to be able to live in such an amazing area

Introduction & Career Goals

From a very young age Tatiana has gravitated twords the art world. She was lucky enough to have an art mentor from the ages of 3 - 17 who guided her into a clear direction within the arts. Tatiana is now a mixed media artist specializing in hyper-realistic portraits, still lifes and landscapes. Upon graduating highschool she knew she wanted a job within the art field but she also wanted to pursue a career that involved technology so she decided on visual communication design. She is happy with her career path and hopes to someday work as an inhouse designer for a tech company.Her passion for art, design and technology only continues to grow and she can't wait to see what the future holds for her.

Passions In Life

Besides her love of the arts, Tatiana loves to spend time in nature and enjoys gardening, and going on hikes. She currently works as a designer for a Carmel art gallery and loves her job.
